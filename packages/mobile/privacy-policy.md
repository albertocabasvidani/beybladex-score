# Privacy Policy - Beyblade X Score Tracker

**Effective Date**: 18/05/2026

## Data Stored Locally

The app stores ONLY on your device:
- Current game scores
- Player names entered
- Game settings (e.g. win score)
- Status of the optional "Remove Ads" purchase

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
The app shows a banner ad in the victory screen via the Google AdMob SDK. AdMob may collect and share with its advertising partners your **Advertising ID** (resettable Android identifier, not personal) to display ads and measure performance.

- Data collected: Advertising ID
- Purpose: Advertising and marketing
- Encrypted in transit: yes
- Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### RevenueCat
To manage the optional one-time "Remove Ads" in-app purchase, the app uses the RevenueCat SDK, which verifies purchase status against RevenueCat and Google Play servers.

- Data collected: Purchase history
- Purpose: App functionality (purchase validation)
- Encrypted in transit: yes
- RevenueCat privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## What the App Does NOT Do
- No account or sign-up required
- No identifiable personal data collected (real name, email, phone, address)
- No analytics services
- No access to location, contacts, photos, microphone or camera
- The app itself works offline; only the third-party services above require internet

## In-App Purchases
A single optional one-time purchase removes the banner ad permanently. Payment is processed entirely by Google Play and RevenueCat; the developer does not receive or store your payment data.

## Minors
The app is rated for all ages (PEGI 3 / Everyone), but the declared target audience is 13+. We do not intentionally collect data from children under 13.
